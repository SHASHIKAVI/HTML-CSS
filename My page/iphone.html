Apple introduces new iphone

This Year New joy begins, New Year-New iphone 11.

The iPhone 11 is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It belongs to the thirteenth generation of iPhones, succeeding the iPhone XR.

iphone 11 has New Airdrop Feature

Here are some key details about the iPhone 11:-


Design and Display

The iPhone 11 features a gorgeous all-screen Liquid Retina LCD display.
It is water-resistant up to 2 meters for up to 30 minutes.
The display size is 6.1 inches with a resolution of 1792 x 828 pixels.
Other display features include True Tone, Wide Color Display (P3), and Haptic Touch.


Camera:-

The iPhone 11 boasts an incredible camera system.
It has an ultra-wide 13 mm lens with a 120-degree field of view, allowing you to capture more scenes.
The 26 mm wide lens provides up to 100% Autofocus in low light conditions.


Performance:-

The iPhone 11 is powered by the A13 Bionic chip.

which ensures smooth multitasking and performance.
It features Face ID, a secure facial authentication method that doesn’t store or share your photo.


Battery Life:-

The phone offers all-day power with its Fast Charge capable 20 W adapter sold separately.
